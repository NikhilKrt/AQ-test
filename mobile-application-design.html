Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do you design an app?

    • Fifth, sending the designed app ahead for development.
  • What is the difference between UI and UX Design?

    UX design refers to user experience design and UI design refers to the user interface design. In terms of usefulness, UI is basically what things look like and UX is the way things work. UX is a procedure, while UI is a deliverable.

  • How much AquGen charge for UI/UX design service?

    As AquGen offers customized services packages, the cost of our services rests on your requirements and your budget. Therefore, we perform tasks accordingly and that’s how the billing is also concluded.

  • Why UX is necessary if we can just design UI?

    UX is necessary because it shows the path of how users get engaged with the app and also improves the overall experience of the app along with the interaction with the product. UI, on the other hand, is responsible for the appearance of the app. So, basically, UX is tied in with making an item functional and helpful, whereas UI is making an item delightful and intuitive.

  • How long will it take to design a mobile app?

    The time taken to complete an app design depends on the complexity and type of the project. On average designing a simple app will take around 2 to 3 weeks and this time can go up to 9 to 10 weeks for a complex app with many features.
