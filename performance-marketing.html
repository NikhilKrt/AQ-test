Performance/Affiliate Marketing - Lead Generation, CPC

Affiliate marketing is fundamentally similar to PPC marketing. To legitimize the process, we ask various different websites to publicize your products and services. You need to pay the affiliates every time they administer internet users to your website. These websites act as affiliates that sign up to affiliate marketing programs and get a commission when referrals create sales. This is a very cost-effective marketing technique as it is dependent on PPC, PPL, and PPS (pay-per-sale).

The idea that affiliate marketing is just for colossal websites with a great number of unique visitors is a relic of days gone by. In the affiliate marketing strategy, you can get a commission in PPL (pay-per-lead) for each lead model, however, the compensation PPC model is significantly more common.
