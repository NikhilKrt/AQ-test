Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the cost of AquGen’s content marketing services?

    As AquGen offers customized content marketing campaign packages, the cost of our services absolutely depends on your requirements and likewise your monthly budget. The final billing is based on the tasks performed by us.

  • How can AquGen help me in planning content?

    Content planning will improve your outcomes and, as an additional advantage, encourages you to allocate resources all the more proficiently. You can spot ways to improve your creation procedure and take advantage of what you have. To truly push for the success of your business, you have to set up how you will utilize content to focus on your target audience and how this can be lined up with the objectives of your business.

  • Do I need content marketing for my business?

    Regardless of whether you are marketing to businesses (B2B) or customers (B2C), you can get benefit from content marketing:

    • Enhances your brand visibility and recognition.
    • Lasting relationships with customers.
    • Opens channels of communication.
    • Builds authority and credibility.
  • Which is better: Short-form content or Long-form content?

    You can decide whether short-form content is better or long-form content by figuring out how much your customers know about you and what you are offering. If your viewers need more instructions about your products or services then long-form content will be the best for you. In cases, where your customers are aware of your products and brand then you should go for short-form content.

  • Is the creation of content regularly really effective?

    Yes, regularly creating the content is really effective, as it is good to keep a constant engagement with your customers. And to make this happen, you need to push out new content routinely. Many marketers talk about the importance of engagement and how it is nothing without consistency. Creating reliable content is probably the greatest test for organizations. Businesses that produce engaging and consistent content, probably see the best benefits.

  • Are keywords important in content marketing?

    Yes, keywords do matter in every type of content marketing you do. Your keywords on YouTube videos, articles, websites, blogs, etc help in reaching people who are searching for businesses online. The number of people who search for this keyword each day enhances the traffic and has a significant impact on organic page rank in search engines.

  • How can we create engaging content for the target audience?

    Here are some points you need to know to create engaging content:

    • Be original.
    • Produce relevant and accurate content.
    • Catchy headlines with the utilization of visuals.
    • Be unique and hook your reader from the start.
  • Can we get conversion through content marketing?

    Yes, you can get conversion through content marketing. You can make your CTAs persuasive and create a sense of urgency in your content. Great content will definitely attract audiences and generate leads. Once you capture those leads, you can advertise your products and services and turn them into sales or conversions.
