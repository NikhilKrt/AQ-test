Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a landing page and why is it significant?

    A landing page is a page on your website that is designed to convert visitors into leads. A landing page is the first impression, so it needs to highlight all the products and services in a very eye-catching manner to leave a lasting impression on the customers. It works in a way when a customer sees CTA and ends up on a landing page with a form, after filling that form customer gets converted into leads. From the information, you can market the lead based on what you know about them.

  • What should be included on a landing page?

    A landing page must have catchy headlines and sub-headlines along with necessary information that shows the path of CTA to the visitors. Highlighting the benefits of what is being offered via one supporting image or a short video attracts more and more visitors and urges them to make CTA.

  • How will AquGen help in developing a landing page?

    In order to get more page visits and new customer, we at AquGen develops mobile-responsive landing pages. We also make landing pages for Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn to make you stand out from your competitors and attracts the attention of your potential customers.

  • Do landing pages rank on Google?

    Yes, landing pages do rank on Google. Following these simple steps landing page may rank on the top of Google.

    • Publish to a custom URL.
    • Plan the keywords.
    • Include your keywords strategically on your landing page.
    • Secure backlinks to your page.
    • Increase the speed of your page.
    • Make your content shareable.
  • Will landing pages undermine my SEO?

    No, landing pages don’t undermine your SEO. It happens only when you are not able to optimize your landing page effectively. You need to make sure what you add to your landing page, with what links to and who it links to. A landing page is a place where you send targeted PPC traffic.

  • What is the difference between a landing page and a website?

    A website is an entire online domain that usually includes various separate pages and posts, whereas a landing page is just one single page on that website. The purposes of the landing pages are also different from that of websites.

  • Do I need a website for a landing page?

    No, as a landing page is a stand-alone page, this means a website is not required for landing pages.
