Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does content marketing strategy help a business?

    A content marketing strategy will help you build a stronger relationship with your target group customers, capture their attention, improve engagement, and improve brand recall. It helps in developing an opinion of your brand amongst customers and if your content is engaging, educational, and valuable, then your audience may start to think the same things about your business.

  • How should I get started with content marketing?

    You can get started with a content marketing strategy by keeping in mind these 3 points:

    • Decide why you need a content marketing strategy
    • Determine your target audience
    • Select appropriate content marketing metrics
  • How do you make engaging content?

    To make engaging content, you need to have the following elements:

    • Sub-headings
    • Proper keywords
    • Should tell a story
    • A particular voice
    • An interesting title
    • Should be shareable
  • From the SEO point of view, which is better: short-form or long-form content?

    If you have aims like increasing traffic from organic searches, then long-form content is the best option. And if your audience is already well-established and you have interesting content to command shares, then short-form can help you remain visible for your potential customers.

  • Is it necessary to create new content regularly?

    You need to regularly add new content to make more pages visible in the search results. This will ensure more people finding your website that can result in a significant increase in traffic. And you need the traffic to order to sell your products and increase brand awareness.

  • Are keywords important? Do you focus on keywords while creating content?

    Yes, keywords are very important in SEO. But, there is also a need to constantly change the way of using keywords. Our AuqGen SEO experts conduct in-depth keyword research to give you a better understanding of the demand for specific keywords and a strategy to compete with competitors and stay ahead of them.

  • Is it necessary for an e-commerce website to have content?

    Yes, it is important for an e-commerce website to have content. E-commerce website users often face problems in acquiring inbound links as most of the websites do not have linking product pages. Therefore, it is important for you to craft link-worthy content.
